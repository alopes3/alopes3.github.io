Web Hosts Research

Here are a few web host providers

GoDaddy

  • Cost: It costs $6.99 a month.
  • Hosting Services: GoDaddy allows you to sell from your web store to in-person, online marketplaces to social platforms. You are given an easy-to-use dashboard to manage your web sales.
  • Domain Services: GoDaddy gives you a domain name, and a website builder upon subscription.
  • Options: GoDaddy has an easy to use interface and provides strong privacy secuirty.

HostGator

  • Cost: It costs $2.75 a month to host a website.
  • Hosting Services: HostGator users get a web domain, a website buidler, and customer support for all website inquiries.
  • Domain Services: HostGator gives users their 1 year free trial for hosting, prevents unauthorize transfers within your domain, email forwarding, and teh ability to manage Ip addresses.
  • Options: HostGator provides free site transfers easy installation, and an SSL security certificate.

FatCow

  • Cost: It costs $4.08 a month to host a website.
  • Hosting Services: Users are given a domain, website building tools, access to blogs and forums,
  • Domain Services: Users are provided with a free domain, with over 370 options to choose from, and unlimited subdomains per account.
  • Options: FatCow provides customer support, and free advertsing on Google and Microsoft.
